## Design Notes

This expansion intentionally avoids large abstract items. Creature figures such as Skoll, Hati, Garmr, Audhumla, Huginn, Muninn, and Fafnir are represented through symbolic props and outfit motifs instead of full animal bodies.

This keeps the image generation stable and prevents:
- extra animal anatomy
- duplicate faces
- cluttered companions
- confusing monster limbs
- scene-heavy backgrounds

## High-Risk Characters and Guards

- Herfjotur: chain must be short and loose, never wrapped around the body.
- Hyrrokkin: wolf identity is a mask only; no funeral gore.
- Garmr: hound collar is held in hand, not worn as restraint.
- Skoll / Hati: wolf masks only, no full wolves.
- Audhumla: pastoral motifs only, no cow body or horns.
- Fafnir: dragon brooch and subtle scale accents only, no wings or tail.
- Dwarf/Smith line: tools stay small and physical, not giant weapons.
