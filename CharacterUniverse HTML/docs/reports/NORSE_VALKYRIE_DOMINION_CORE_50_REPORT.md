## Item Policy

All main items are simple physical objects:
- spear
- hammer
- dagger
- spindle
- necklace
- horn cup
- sword
- horn
- flower
- bow
- apple
- lyre
- shell
- branch
- coin
- cup
- vial
- comb
- banner
- shield
- helmet
- axe
- lantern
- mask
- chain
- ring
- stone
- needle
- crown
- book
- net
- horseshoe charm
- letter

Avoided:
- vague relics
- abstract cosmic objects
- full animal bodies
- monster anatomy clutter
- huge scenery props
- excessive snow/fire/rune effects
- chains or threads wrapping the body

## Higher-Risk Characters

- Fenrir: chain must be loose and symbolic.
- Jormungandr: serpent motif through ring and jewelry only, no giant snake.
- Nidhogg: dragon motif symbolic only, no wings or full dragon body.
- Sleipnir: symbolic horseshoe charm only, no horse body or extra legs.
- Ratatoskr: squirrel traits symbolic through hair ornaments only.
